TagNamePurposeExample

<p>ParagraphBlock of text<p>Hello</p>
<strong>BoldImportant/bold text<strong>Bold</strong>
<em>ItalicEmphasised/italic text<em>Italic</em>

<hr>Horizontal RuleThematic divider line<hr />
<blockquote>BlockquoteIndented quote from another source<blockquote>Quote</blockquote>

Links & Media

Tags for embedding images, videos, and creating hyperlinks.

TagNamePurposeKey Attributes
<a>AnchorHyperlink to another page or URLhref, target
<img>ImageEmbeds an imagesrc, alt, width
<video>VideoEmbeds a video playersrc, controls, autoplay
<audio>AudioEmbeds an audio playersrc, controls

Semantic & Layout

Tags that define the structure and meaning of page sections.

TagPurpose
<header>Top section of a page or component
<nav>Navigation links
<main>Primary content of the page (one per page)
<section>A thematic grouping of content
<article>Self-contained content (blog post, news article)
<aside>Related sidebar content
<footer>Bottom section of a page or component
<div>Generic block container (no semantic meaning)
<span>Generic inline container (no semantic meaning)
